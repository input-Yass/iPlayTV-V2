iPlayTV Store Refund Policy

At iPlaysTV.com, we want to ensure that you are 100% happy with your purchase.

Don’t like what you bought? Read on for how to return your purchase for a refund. If you’ve recently made a purchase through the iPlayTV Store and want a refund, you’re in the right place. Below you’ll find all the info on what you can refund, when you can refund, and how to begin the process.

When are products eligible for a refund?

Products are eligible for refund within 30 days of purchase if marked as "refundable" or "self-refundable". However, you must ensure the compatibility of your device and the quality of your internet before resorting to the refund service.

You will not be eligible for a refund for products that have been banned in your area or that have violated their terms of service, this is beyond our scope of expertise. In addition, you may not be eligible for a refund if iPlayTV determines that you are abusing the refund policy.

How can I request a refund?

For refundable purchases if you have questions about the refund process, you can request a refund by clicking “Contact Us” on our iPlayTV Home page and Launcher help page and submitting a refund request.

How will I get my refund?

By default, purchases will be refunded to the payment method used to make the purchase, but this may not always be possible depending on the payment method. You and iPlayTV may also agree that the refund be issued by alternative method. Please note the amount of time for the refund to process is dependent on your payment method.

What about pre-purchases?

You can cancel your pre-purchase and get a refund at any time prior to the official order date and no later than 24 hours.

What if a product I recently purchased goes on sale?

You can refund your purchase for a full refund and immediately re-purchase the product as long as it abides by the above guidelines. We do not consider this to be refund abuse.

If iPlayTV determines that you are abusing the refund policy, you may not be eligible for a refund. The policy is in place so you can buy with peace of mind and be assured that you are only spending on the tv you love and have fun streaming!
